Sujith Anand

Insights Discovery® Cheat Sheet

Defining Statement:

  • Sujith doesn’t take criticism personally and is often surprised to discover that others may be hurt or offended by the constructive criticism he can offer.
  • Sujith is analytical, impersonal, and interested in underlying principles.
  • Displays very little emotional response to situations and prefers logic over emotion.
  • Will prefer to be reserved and distant until he gets to know someone well.
Sujith's Value To The Team
  • Seeks and provides attention to detail.
  • Helps ensure consistency in team output.
  • Identifies the key elements of important situations.
  • Sometimes takes over the work of others rather than leave important tasks undone or done poorly.
When Communicating With Sujith DO ...
  • Be precise and detailed.
  • Be punctual.
  • Back up your assertions with reasons and data.
  • Be patient if he starts hair-splitting.
When Communicating With Sujith DON’T ...
  • Waste time with irrelevancies.
  • Be vague about facts and statistics.
  • Come unprepared and disorganized.
  • Cut him off before he has finished.
